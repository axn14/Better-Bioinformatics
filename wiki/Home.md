# Better Bioinformatics — Wiki

This wiki is the **concept layer** of the [Better Bioinformatics](https://github.com/axn14/Better-Bioinformatics)
series. It holds the reusable, generalizable guidance — the checklists and mental models that
apply beyond any single example.

The concrete, runnable case studies (code, data, figures) live in the
[`mistakes/`](https://github.com/axn14/Better-Bioinformatics/tree/main/mistakes) folder of the
repository. Each wiki guide links to the case study that motivated it, and vice versa.

## Guides

- **[SHAP Inference Guide](SHAP-Inference-Guide)** — how to make defensible inferences from SHAP:
  precursors to check before you trust it, a step-by-step workflow, a troubleshooting table, and
  post-analysis follow-ups. Motivated by case study
  [01 — SHAP ranking instability](https://github.com/axn14/Better-Bioinformatics/tree/main/mistakes/01-shap-ranking-instability).
